Menu

Our coffee is driven by a single word: simplicity (also quality, but that’s obvious, isn’t it?). Indeed, there are enough cafe with menus longer than the arms of their maker. Here, we keep it simple, because it allows us to stay focused on the quality of our coffee: we know what you want to offer, and we do not go back our ways. It is also thanks to a simple recipe that our maker allow themselves to innovate certain menu more often and to update our ingredient on a regular basis.

Is there a universe where it is possible to drink coffee made with love and passion? Where we don't even ask you if you want tiramisu for dessert (because we know you want it)?
